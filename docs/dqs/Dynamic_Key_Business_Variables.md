## Key Structural Observations at Starting Point

**Primary asset:** Domain network spanning OEM and supplier sides, with 4 non-competing pilots in the same segment who can share common customer inputs. This is rare and difficult to replicate.

**Primary constraint:** Zero cash, zero full-time commitment. Everything must be sequenced, not parallelized.

**Primary risk / single point of dependency:** Pilot A formal commitment. Without real project data from Pilot A, all prototypes remain experience-based and unvalidated. C is engaging Pilot A's CEO directly to secure this.

**Primary market tailwind:** Jan 2027 AIAG & VDA FMEA implementation deadline + Excel-dominated industry + fastest-growing APQP market region (ASEAN) + no AI-native competitor in the space.
